Who we are

We’re people who like drinking coffee (or tea or other; we’re openminded)
and being outside. Most of us are also people who really like commuting
by bicycle. In fact, this event is organized by the Corvallis Bicycle Collective.

Ask-A-Wrench trailer

Nothing goes worse with coffee than an annoying rubbing sound that your bike
shouldn’t be making. Corvallis Bicycle Collective’s Ask-A-Wrench trailer will be
on scene for quick bike fixes in support of the optimal coffee experience.
